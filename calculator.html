How this is calculated

This is a planning estimate built on widely cited sales benchmarks, not a guarantee. After hours capture assumes the share of inquiries that arrive nights and weekends currently go unanswered until the next business day, and that answering them instantly recovers conversions at a 20% uplift. Speed to lead lift applies a smaller conversion gain to your daytime leads, scaled to how quickly you reply today, since a faster first response makes a lead far more likely to convert. Staff hours reclaimed assumes roughly fifteen minutes of staff time per inquiry for the first response and qualification that the AI now handles. Adjust any input to see how the numbers move.
